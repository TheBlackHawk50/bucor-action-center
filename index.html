Mission

To protect the public by safekeeping and reforming persons deprived of liberty.

Vision

A modernized and credible institution composed of highly trained and motivated personnel implementing humane safekeeping and development programs for persons deprived of liberty for their reintegration into society.

  • Integrity
  • Professionalism
  • Teamwork
