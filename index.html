Mission

To protect the public by safekeeping and reforming persons deprived of liberty (PDLs) through responsive and efficient rehabilitation programs.

Vision

A modernized and professional corrections system that promotes the reformation of PDLs and their reintegration into society as law-abiding citizens.

Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Accountability
  • Respect for Human Rights
  • Professionalism
  • Service Excellence
